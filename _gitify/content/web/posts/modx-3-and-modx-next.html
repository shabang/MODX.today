We've been regularly posting about the future of MODX. From providing links to Jason Coward's articles on Medium, to a summary of what was known and ideas from the community about what the future might, or should, look like. 

One thing that was failed to properly communicate - even by MODX.today! - is the difference between MODX 3 and MODX Next. These are not new terms, but people seem to think they're the same, while they're not. 

MODX 3 is the next logical evolution for MODX Revolution. It's a release that will have breaking changes from MODX Revolution 2.x, but unnecessary breaking changes will be avoided. It's a chance to upgrade MODX to the next level by removing legacy and making things better, however it's not what Jason has been writing about.
