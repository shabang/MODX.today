Hello MODXers!

Today is the last day to get your Early Bird tickets for the MODXpo in Munich! We also have two new speakers, let me introduce to you a couple of experienced MODX community members ready to share their know-how:

Ivan Klimchuk

He is founder of the local MODX community (modx.by) in Belarus, and a MODX Ambassador. His small company Alroniks Experts provides MODX development services and other web-technologies. They also develop and sell custom MODX Extras.

Ivan will be telling the story of how through the development of one small e-commerce Extra, "Minishop", that the Russian community was able to expand and build up an ecosystem consisting of more than 50 Extras. This success also brought about the release of the highly acclaimed Minishop2.

Romain Tripault

Romain is a freelance MODX developer that is well-known in the community, and has been creating MODX CMPs (Custom Manager Pages) for a very long time. He will be sharing his secrets by telling us two different stories. One goes down the PHP development path, showing us the proper structure with which a PHP developer can create a CMP in MODX. While the other path shows us how to create CMPs with the use of just traditional MODX Elements, such as Chunks and Snippets. Come join and learn how to create powerful Custom Manager Pages.

Join us at MODXpo!

There are still early bird tickets left! Don't hesitate and order your ticket before the regular price kicks in tomorrow.

MODXpo 2015 Sponsors

We would like to thank all of our generous sponsors: Sterc, GEL Studios, modmore, chsmedien, BD Creative, Kochan & Partner and of course MODX!

We are starting to reach out to new partners and sponsors. If you are interested in helping support the most anticipated MODXpo event of the year, please read our prospectus, and find out what opportunities you can have at MODXpo. Sponsoring starts as low as 100 EUR!!!

Yours truly, on behalf of the entire MODXpo organizing team,
