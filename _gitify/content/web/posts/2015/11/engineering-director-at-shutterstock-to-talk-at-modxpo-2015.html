Kiran Patel is an Engineering Director at Shutterstock, managing teams within Shutterstock''s Enterprise and Editorial business. He is an experienced technologist with a track record of building and scaling an idea to a multi-million dollar business. With more than a decade of experience in Web, Mobile, Wireless and Media industries, Kiran worked at companies ranging from small and innovative startups to large corporations and received awards of excellence for his work. Kiran has a Bachelor degree in Computer Science from the University of Mumbai.<\/p>

Organizations are looking into software solutions which cater to their current as well as future needs. The recent trend is to break one size fits-all monolithic software applications into small domain specific micro services. In this talk he will be covering the following topics:<\/p>

      Kiran Patel is an Engineering Director at Shutterstock, managing teams within Shutterstock's Enterprise and Editorial business. He is an experienced technologist with a track record of building and scaling an idea to a multi-million dollar business. With more than a decade of experience in Web, Mobile, Wireless and Media industries, Kiran worked at companies ranging from small and innovative startups to large corporations and received awards of excellence for his work. Kiran has a Bachelor degree in Computer Science from the University of Mumbai.

      Software is changing the world. In today's ever changing technological advancements, its very important for businesses to have their long-term vision and business strategies aligned with their business process. Organizations are looking into software solutions which cater to their current as well as future needs. The recent trend is to break one size fits-all monolithic software applications into small domain specific micro services. In this talk he will be covering the following topics:

      • What is Growth?
      • How we build the software today?
      • Challenges with Monolith Application
      • Prepare your Organization for Transition
      • Device a Transition Plan
