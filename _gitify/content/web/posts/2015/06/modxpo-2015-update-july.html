We would like to introduce three fantastic new speakers that are ready to give energetic performances that you will love, plus give you a little more info about the event.

As you know, this year's MODXpo takes place on November 21-22nd in Munich, Germany, at the conference hall of the Munich design agency, Kochan & Partner. The conference is two days, each day offering one track of seminars running from the morning to early evening. Topics include best practices in regards to front-end tooling, back-end best practices in areas of Extras creation and deployment, MODX security, improving your site setup workflow, user and group permissions configuration, and much more.

There will also be plenty of time to meet new people and learn from other MODXers. we've got a room dedicated to code sharing and idea exchanging, while outside we will be offering a pavilion to give attendees a place to take a break and get some fresh air. Less formal socializing is also an important part of MODXpo 2015 and, therefore, we're hosting a "chill and lounge" evening on Saturday night.

We will announce ticket prices soon and special, and very limited, early-bird pricing. So, stay tuned for that!

So, who have we selected to speak at MODXpo 2015? We thought you'd never ask!

Gauke Pieter Sietzema

Gauke is the Co-founder and Lead developer of Sterc, a Dutch internet & marketing Agency. Sterc maintains various MODX extras like SEO Pro, SEO Tab, Formit and the Google Analytics Dashboard. Gauke will be diving into the Slim framework, which will be the foundation for MODX 3.0!

Graeme Leighfield

GEL Studios have been leading the way in creating highly bespoke, web, marketing and digital solutions for a wide variety of clients all across the UK, and at their core, is MODX. Specialising in user training and knowledge retention, Graeme will be talking about how we can better use the MODX manager and its available tools, to ensure user administration satisfaction - guaranteed!

Menno Pietersen

Menno Pietersen is a responsive web design and MODX development specialist. His company is Any Screen Size where he also blogs about web design, development and MODX. Menno also loves to design and develop MODX themes, these are available at MPThemes. When Menno is not online he is probably having fun with his 1 year old daughter Fenna or enjoying the finer things in life.

We are proud to announce that GEL Studios has joined our list of sponsors. GEL Studios is a MODX agency making brilliant looking websites for small and medium sized enterprises. Check out their website!

Our other invaluable sponsors and event partners are BD Creative, chsmedien, modmore, and MODX. If you would like to sponsor us, please get in touch with Jay Gilmore (jay@modx.com) at MODX and be put on the list!

Yours truly, on behalf of the entire MODXpo organizing team,

Benjamin Davis
