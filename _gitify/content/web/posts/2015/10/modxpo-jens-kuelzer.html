Jens Külzer formerly worked as an IT consultant where he planned and implemented monitoring systems for Microsoft infrastructures in large DAX companies (up to thousands of servers). After becoming self-employed and switching his focus from servers to web, he is now on a mission to draw any webworkers' attention to a more professional service operation for their sites.

His talk is about Service Operations as part of an ITIL-like good practice. MODX and the community focus a lot on the ease of creating new sites and new features inside of those sites. Once a site is "in the wild" maintaining it becomes time consuming and not well enough supported by the systems themselves.

In this talk he will provide everyday examples where automatic, continuous monitoring saves you time and increases security and customer experience. This is a plea for more professionalism. At the end (if enough time left), he would like to demonstrate a preview of a new service that he is currently working on which completely monitors your (MODX) web systems and gives you a cockpit overview of all your sites.
