Please welcome the newest member of the MODX.today family: Release Robot Robbie!

As the name might indicate, Release Robot Robbie is not entirely human. He's a script, about 400 lines long, that has been tasked to automatically keep you up to date on new releases of MODX extras. 

To do this, Release Robot Robbie regularly chats with various MODX package providers, including modx.com and modmore.com. He checks if there are any new versions of MODX packages available, and if so, he adds that information to a post on MODX.today. 

Just to make sure we don't get a list with hundreds of releases, Release Robot Robbie creates a new post every week, typically on Monday or the first day of the week there is an actual update available. Over the course of the week, Robbie updates the post with any new releases. 

His first post is published right now, and you can track all of his writing via his author page.
