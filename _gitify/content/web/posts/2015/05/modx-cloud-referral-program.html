In a recent blog post, the MODX Cloud team announced they are launching a pilot for a Referral Program. This program will provide agencies building websites for clients a new source of potential revenue by referring their clients to the MODX Cloud Service. 

About MODX Cloud

For those unaware, MODX Cloud is a hosting platform and development toolkit for websites built on MODX. It boosts some really powerful features including the ability to take snapshots of entire sites, and create complete copies with just a few clicks. Upgrades are also dealt with in just a few clicks from the dashboard, making developing and maintaining a MODX site easy. 

On top of that, the hosting is tuned specifically for MODX, which makes it run faster than a large part of generic hosts out there. 

Revenue Sharing

The referral program pilot the team is starting with, offers two options for revenue sharing for each newly referred paying clients.

With the first option, the first two monthly payments are paid out to the referrer. This is an interesting approach where there is an immediate substantial referral payout. The second option is to receive 10% of the revenue in the long term, which is a more traditional model. 

Interested, or want to know more?

For more information and to apply to the program, head over to the Referral Program page on the MODX Cloud website.
